SIT-UP

Toes and heels together. Flex your feet, Lock your legs, Keep your heels on the floor.
Arms over your head, Cross your thumbs, Arms and head together.
Inhale breathing, Quickly sit up.
Exhale breathing, Grab your big toes, Double jerk.
Touch your forehead to the knees, Elbows to the floor.
Turn around and lie on your stomach for the spine strengthening series.
