LOCUST POSE
Salabhasana

Lie on your stomach.
Arms straight position, Get your arms underneath your body.
Hands / palms facing the floor.
Elbows completely underneath your stomach, Invisible.
Fingers completely apart. Grab the floor with your fingertips.
Two little baby fingers touching side by side.
Chin on the floor.
Left leg relaxed. Right leg solid, concrete, one piece, pointed toes.
Inhale breathing.
Slowly, Gently, Right leg lift up from the floor, Minimum 45 degrees, Half of 90.
Go up, Much higher everybody.
Leg up more, Lift up more, A couple of inches.
Lock the knee, Pointed toes, More up.
And change. Slowly, Right leg down, And left leg up… (repeat for the left leg.)
(BOTH LEGS):
Turn your head in, Mouth on the floor.
Readjust your hands / palms. Elbows closer together underneath the body.
Open your fingers, Grab the floor with your fingertips.
Full lungs, Take a deep breath, And
Without bending your knees, Both legs up.
Come up please. Everybody go up, Come up, Everybody come up.
Lock the knees, Feet together.
Come up. Take a deep breath, Come up more.
And slowly, Both legs down.
Relax please. Arms by your side, Turn your head to the side.
(2nd Set):
You can count up to 10 seconds, with corrections in between.
(additional dialogue):
Hands closer to each other –
Two little pinkies should be touching each other, Side by side,
Completely underneath the body, Invisible.
Do not lift or turn your head throughout the posture.
(single legs):
(In the set up): If your elbows are bent and underneath the body,
Bring your arms out beside the body and straighten them.
Keep the legs exactly behind your head in the mirror.
Make sure your hip doesn’t twist out of line.
(both legs):
Use your hand strength against the floor, To get your legs up more.
