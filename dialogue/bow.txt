BOW POSE
Dhanurasana

Chin on the floor.
Hold your feet from the outside, Exactly 2 inches below the toes.
Five fingers together – Make sure thumbs touching with the index fingers.
Please don’t lose the grip.
Six inches apart between the knees and the toes.
Wrists straight, Pointed toes.
Arms and legs should look like 2 wheels in one base.
Take a deep breath, And gently both legs kick up toward the ceiling.
Look up and kick up.
Kick your legs back.
Continuously keep kicking, Don’t stop kicking.
Roll forward, Until your toes are in the center from the side.
Kick back more, Harder.
Head up more.
Kick your legs behind you.
Wrists straight more.
Keep your knees in, Pointed toes.
Kick back more.
Kick harder.
Slowly lower down, And you Relax.
Completely Relax.
