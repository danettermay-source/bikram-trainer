HALF MOON BACKBEND

Next is backward bending.
Take a deep breath, Full lungs.
Drop your head back as far as it goes, Look at the floor behind you.
Arms back immediately, Try to touch the wall behind you.
Bend your spine backwards, Coccyx to the neck.
Lower back, Middle back, Upper back, Total spine backward bending.
Push your upper body back, Try to fall down backwards.
Maximum body weight in the heels.
Inhale breathing, Push your stomach, Legs, Hips, Thighs.
Everything forward toward the mirror.
Arms back, Look back, Fall back, Way back, Go back, More back.
Inhale breathing, Come back up, Stop in the middle.
Keep your arms there.
