FIXED FIRM POSE
Supta-Vajrasana

Come to the top of the towel.
Sit down, Kneeling position.
Separate your feet and sit down between the heels,
Hips touching the floor.
Heels touching the hips.
Make sure the heels are touching the hips the whole time.
If your knees or feet hurt, You can open your knees.
Put your hands-palms on your toes, Thumbs inside, Fingers outside.
Touch your right elbow on the floor, Then the left, One by one.
Touch your head on the floor, Then the back of your head.
Your shoulders should touch the floor.
Whole upper body relaxed on the floor.
Bring your arms over the head.
Grab your elbows, Over the head.
Pull your elbows down toward the floor.
Chin down toward the chest, Neck flat on the floor.
Lift your chest and stomach up toward the ceiling,
Create a perfect human bridge.
Eventually, Bring your knees together, Touching each other.
Make sure knees never come off the floor.
Eyes open, Breathing is normal.
Put your hands on your feet, With the help of your elbows, Carefully come up.
Turn around and relax on your back.
